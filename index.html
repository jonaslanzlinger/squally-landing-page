Hi, I'm Squally!

Hey! I am Squally and I am more than just a guide — I'm your personal companion to explore and connect with the art and stories woven into our campus. This project was created to help you uncover hidden gems, learn about the artists behind each piece, and see our campus in a whole new way.

With Squally, you'll discover a curated selection of artworks spread across campus, each with its unique story and meaning. Whether you’re a student, faculty member, or visitor, we hope Squally adds a spark of inspiration to your day, making each step a little more colorful and meaningful.

We appreciate feedback of your experience and interaction with Squally so that we can constantly improve Squally’s capabilities of offering an unique art experience at the University of St. Gallen. To give feedback please follow this Feedback Form !
